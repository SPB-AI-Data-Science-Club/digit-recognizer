How It Works
  1. Your strokes are cropped to their bounding box
  2. Scaled to 20x20 and centered by pixel mass in a 28x28 field, exactly how MNIST digits are normalized
  3. A convolutional network maps the image to 10 class scores
  4. Softmax turns scores into the probability bars on the right
Architecture. Two 3x3 convolution blocks (16 and 32 filters) with max pooling, then a 64-unit dense layer with dropout. Trained with Adam on the 60,000-image MNIST training set.
